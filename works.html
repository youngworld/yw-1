Meanwhile

"Meanwhile" is a publishing prototype for filmmakers and literary journalists who are interested in presenting non-linear narratives with multiple pathways and simultaneous viewpoints by which to more deeply explore a given idea, event, or individual.

Recess

For thirty minutes a day, kids slip into their own, sometimes imaginary worlds and reign free. They jump, run, tag, and pretend without fear. "Recess" is an original film series that follows social play amongst kids in diverse settings, supplemented by an interactive catalog of “recess" games, and what they can mean to those who play them.

The Beginners

In this original film series, we meet a group of highly “successful” individuals, from the wunderkinds to the well-established, who invite us into a domain they’ve mastered over the years, but then reveal something else they each wish that they had explored further or for the very first time.

This is not a series about past regrets but about people actively pursuing an interest, intimate and long overdue, in their next act of life. How will the experience penetrate their professional lives? Following their humbling, first-person odyssey of self-renewal, we watch kings and queens become beginners once again.

Shoveler

"Shoveler" is a concept for an iOS application that sends and receives messages according to location, not time. Rather than feel a false sense of urgency in our world, what if messages waited for you at their optimal location? How might this simple shift impact not only the types of messages we send, and to whom, but also our relationship to the physical environment?
